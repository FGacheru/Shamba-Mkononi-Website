ABOUT US At Shamba Mkononi we envision a future filled with vibrant farms that are able to meet the needs of the comunity. We believe using technologies like GIS and precision farming we can help build human capacity, revitalize communities, supply regional food systems, and gradually eliminate famine and food insecurity.
We work with various partners whose vision and purpose align with ours of ensuring that we eradicate hunger and work towards fulfiling the UN-SDG Goal 2; Zero hunger. That said the bulk of our projects have been in Nyeri county, Kirinyaga county and Uasin Gishu county as they are the counties that produce the bulk of food in the country. We are however committed to expanding our boundaries and equiping more farmers with the knowledge, expertise and resources required for them to florish.
We pride ourselves in not only offering practical ways to farm sustainably but also offer resources to the farmers ever so often that enable them to keep upbreast withup to date knowledge on best farming resources. This is possible through the use of GIS technology that allows us to carry out Precision farming by the use of remote sensing technology as ell as carrrying out suitabilty analysis. The use of this technologies allows us to provide the farmers with data that is precise to the soil profile, climatic condition, land demacation, metrological reports among other aspects that allow the farmers to get the maximum results from their farms. Maps, resource people, up to date data bases are among the resources that we offer our farmers.
